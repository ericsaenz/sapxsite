About Us

 We are a newly-formed indie game studio/startup based in Baltimore, MD, co-founded by Johns Hopkins, Peabody, and Haverford undergraduates.

 Our leading members have been experienced indie game developers, professional composers, passionate gamers, and have taken on project management & publishing roles in top-earning firms in the game industry.

 Our motto is "Adventure, Advocacy, Art." We aim to revolutionize how indie games are made and distributed, and we hope to create commercially-successful indie games with a social advocacy focus.
